A fully asynchronous training infrastructure for large-scale agent reinforcement learning. Features **100+ hot-pluggable tools** with unified sandbox management, **20+ benchmark one-click evaluation**, and a **full-cycle visualization platform**. The database-centric architecture decouples inference sampling from policy training, supporting multi-task parallel sampling, continuous training updates, and unified multi-turn trajectory modeling. **Significantly outperforms Verl** and other open-source agent RL frameworks in GPU utilization and training speed.
