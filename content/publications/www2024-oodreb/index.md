abstract: 'Relation extraction (RE) methods have achieved striking performance when training and test data are i.i.d. However, in real-world scenarios, the assumption can hardly be satisfied. We serve as the first effort to study OOD problems in RE by constructing OODREB and investigating SOTA RE methods. Our benchmark reveals that existing methods struggle in both i.i.d. and OOD settings, fail to learn causality, and are far from deployment in real-world applications.'
